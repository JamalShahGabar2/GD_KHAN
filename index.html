ABOUT ME

I'm an Assistant Professor in Development Economics at Hiroshima University

I specialize in empirical development economics with a focus on migration, rural development, agriculture, and extractive industries. My work uses causal inference techniques to design evidence-based policies for fragile and low-income economies.
