About Dr. Ghulam Dastgir Khan

Dr. Ghulam Dastgir Khan is an Assistant Professor at Hiroshima University's Graduate School of Innovation and Practice for Smart Society (SmaSo). His research focuses on empirical development economics, with particular attention to migration, rural development, agriculture, and extractive industries. Dr. Khan is dedicated to generating empirical evidence to support policymakers in addressing complex development challenges. Through his research, he aims to make meaningful contributions to the advancement of development policy.
